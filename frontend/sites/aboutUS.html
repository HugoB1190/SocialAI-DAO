Our Story

Founded in 2020 by AI researchers and open-source enthusiasts, Fignite emerged from a simple idea: what if artificial intelligence development was driven by community needs rather than corporate agendas?

Starting as a small team of 5, we've grown into a global movement of 500+ contributors across 40 countries, delivering cutting-edge AI solutions while maintaining our commitment to transparency and accessibility.
